ShadowDeck™ Range

Fasteners

ShadowDeck™ Fasteners

Made from UV-resistant, high-strength nylon and designed for outdoor conditions, our fasteners resist sun damage and maintain their strength over time.

3mm Hidden Deck Fastener

Used with Kwila pre-grooved timber, creating a consistent 3mm space between boards.

T-Shaped Fastener

Joins the boundary board to the ends of the decking boards for a clean junction.

80mm Butt-Joint Double Screw Fastener

Wider fastener that picks up the edge of two boards where they meet at a joint.

100mm Butt-Joint Biscuit

Ensures decking and picture frame boards sit at the same height and adds strength to joints.

12mm Deck-2-House Spacer

Creates a clean 12mm space between the house and the deck, and picks up the first board.

SPAX Stainless Screws 6g 50mm

Made in Germany, specifically designed for hardwood decking applications.

Kwila Timber

ShadowDeck™ Kwila Timber Profiles

Premium Kwila Timber Profiles designed to pair with the ShadowDeck™ Nail-Free system. Profiles shown are indicative and not to scale.

140x21mm Domed Decking Board

2-sided groove. Our most common product. Domed for water shedding and grooved both sides for fast nail-free fixing.

140x21mm Bull Nose Picture Frame & Step Profile

1-sided groove. Perimeter board for a picture-frame effect, also used on step treads.

140x21mm Picture Frame & Step Profile

1-sided groove. For picture framing the deck. The groove underneath the perimeter edge picks up the fascia board.

140x21mm Flat Decking Profile

2-sided groove. Traditional flat finish, 140mm wide, for a best-in-class result.

180x19mm Fascia Profile

Multipurpose fascia profile, 19x180mm, to finish the outside edge of your deck.

Lineal Metres Calculator

To calculate the amount of decking you need in lineal metres, multiply the width of the deck by the length. For a full breakdown including wastage and fascia boards, see our Installation Guide.
