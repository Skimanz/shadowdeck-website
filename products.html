Fasteners

ShadowDeck™ Fasteners – UV Nylon, Strong and Durable

Our ShadowDeck™ Fasteners are made from high-strength, UV-resistant nylon for exceptional durability in outdoor conditions. Designed to securely hold decking boards without visible nails or screws, these fasteners resist sun damage and maintain their strength over time, ensuring a long-lasting, sleek finish for your deck.

ShadowDeck™ 3mm Hidden Deck Fastener

Our 3mm Fastener is designed to be used with our Kwila pre-grooved timber to allow a 3mm space between boards.

ShadowDeck™ T Shaped Fastener

Designed to join boundary board to the ends of your decking boards.

ShadowDeck™ 80mm Butt-Joint Double Screw Fastener

Designed wider to pick up the edge of two boards at once where there is a joint.

ShadowDeck™ 100mm Butt-Joint Biscuit

Will ensure the decking & picture frame boards are at the same height and adds strength to the joints.

ShadowDeck™ 12mm Deck-2-House Spacer

Creates a clean 12mm space between the house and the deck, and picks up the first board.

SPAX Stainless Screws 6g 50mm

Made in Germany, specifically designed for hardwood decking applications.

Kwila Timber

ShadowDeck™ Kwila Timber Profiles

Explore our premium range of Kwila Timber Profiles, expertly designed to pair perfectly with the ShadowDeck™ Nail-Free Timber Decking System. These profiles are crafted to provide an exceptional, seamless finish, enhancing the natural beauty of your deck while ensuring durability and long-lasting performance. With our patented nail-free system, you'll achieve a sleek, polished look that elevates any outdoor space.

Profiles are indicative and not to scale

140x21mm Domed Decking Board, 2 sided groove

This is the most common decking product we sell. Domed to allow the water to shed, grooved on both sides to make for a fast and efficient nail free fixing.

140x21mm Bull Nose Picture Frame & Step Profile, 1 sided groove

Design to be used on a perimeter board to give it the picture frame effect as well as on the tread of a step.

140x21mm Picture Frame & Step Profile, 1 sided groove

Mainly used to picture frame the deck. Has a groove underneath the perimeter edge to pick up the facia board.

140x21mm Flat Decking Profile, 2 sided groove

We also provide a 21mm flat decking profile with 2 sided groove for the more traditional finish. These 140mm wide boards give a best in class finish.

180x19mm Facia Profile

A multipurpose facia profile 18x180mm.

Lineal Metres Calculator

To calculate the amount of decking you need in lineal metres, multiply the width of the deck by the length. For full details including wastage allowance, see our Installation Guide.
