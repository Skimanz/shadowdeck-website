How it works

The SuperSpan System

ShadowDeck™ SuperSpan is an innovative aluminium framing system which can be used with decking and tiles. The clever design lets you change from tiles to decking without changing your framing. Just flip the aluminium joist and insert the fasteners for tiles or a nail-free timber solution.

Layouts

Floating Aluminium Deck Frame Layouts

The FlippnClick™ aluminium framing system has key advantages over traditional timber framing. Its simplicity and lightweight design means even apprentices can quickly master it. A key feature is the ability to click the frame directly into a self-levelling Nurajack head — making installation 80% faster than timber, while still offering an incredibly stable surface with wide spans between jacks.

Joist Layout

Designed for spaces with low clearance. The joists are clipped directly into a Nurajack at 1200 centres.

Joist & Bearer Layout

Designed to be extra strong. Joists are fixed to the bearer using Joist to Bearer Side Clips, and the bearers clip into a Nurajack at 1200 centres.

Components

SuperSpan SubFraming Components

Joist to Bearer Side Clip

Turn the clip into the bearer framing to lock the joist edge securely in place. Can be secured with or without screws.

Joist/Bearer Framing Joiner

Joins and extends framing or bearer aluminium lengths, reinforcing for additional strength.

Joist/Bearer Corner Bracket

For 90-degree angle connections. Slides inside the frame and screws through pre-drilled holes.
