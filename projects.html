Recent Projects

ShadowDeck™ in Action

A selection of completed decking projects using the ShadowDeck™ nail-free system and SuperSpan aluminium sub-framing.

Auckland, New Zealand

The Metropolis, Auckland

A rooftop decking installation at The Metropolis, one of Auckland's most recognised apartment buildings. ShadowDeck™ Kwila timber decking was installed over SuperSpan aluminium framing and Nurajack pedestals, delivering a sleek nail-free finish suited to the building's premium specification. The system's low profile and membrane-safe pedestal design made it ideal for this high-rise rooftop application.

Rooftop deck Nurajack pedestals SuperSpan framing Kwila timber
St Johns, Auckland

Glover Road, St Johns

A residential deck build in St Johns, Auckland. The ShadowDeck™ system was used to create a seamless outdoor living area, with hidden fasteners ensuring a clean, nail-free surface that's comfortable underfoot and built to last. The natural Kwila hardwood finish complements the home's architecture while providing exceptional durability in New Zealand's variable climate.

Residential Outdoor living Kwila timber Hidden fasteners
Freemans Bay, Auckland

Beaumont Quarter, Freemans Bay

A deck installation at Beaumont Quarter in Auckland's Freemans Bay. The project used the ShadowDeck™ SuperSpan aluminium sub-framing system over a waterproof membrane, with Nurajack pedestals providing precise height adjustment and membrane protection. The result is a high-quality timber deck with a refined picture-frame finish suited to this contemporary urban setting.

Over membrane Nurajack pedestals Picture frame finish Urban residential
