What is ShadowDeck™

An Unbeatable Finish, Every Time

ShadowDeck™ is a Nail-Free timber decking system that uses pre-grooved timber and a hidden fastener to secure the decking boards, eliminating the need for visible nails or screws. This creates a sleek, clean surface while allowing the timber to expand and contract naturally with the seasons.

Whether you're creating a seamless outdoor space or a durable, long-lasting deck, ShadowDeck™ offers a high-performance, stylish solution that ensures your timber deck looks great and performs at its best year after year.

Key Features

01

Nail-Free Design

Hidden fasteners create a smooth, clean surface completely free from visible nails or screws.

02

Natural Expansion & Contraction

Allows timber to breathe with temperature and humidity changes, preventing twisting, warping and splitting.

03

Durability

Hidden fasteners ensure a secure fit, while pre-grooved timber provides enhanced stability and strength.

04

Enhanced Aesthetics

With no exposed fasteners, ShadowDeck™ delivers a sleek, refined appearance for modern outdoor spaces.

05

Quick & Easy Installation

Pre-grooved timber and hidden fastener clips make installation faster and easier than traditional decking.

06

Barefoot Friendly

No protruding nails or screws — just a smooth, splinter-free surface that's safe on toes.
