SuperSpan™ Aluminium Span Tables

PS1 certified by EQ Struc Limited. For residential and commercial decking applications. All values comply with NZ Building Code (AS/NZS 1170:2002 & AS/NZS 1664:1997).

How to read these tables: Select your load category, then choose your profile size. For joist-only builds, use the Joist Span column. For bearer + joist builds, cross-reference your joist span with the bearer span. Values shown as simply supported / continuous*. Minimum back span = 4× overhang. Joist spacing: 450mm centres (400mm for educational facilities).
