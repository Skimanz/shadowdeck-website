How will your deck be supported?
The support system determines your deck height and whether Nurajack pedestals are included in your estimate.

Height-adjustable aluminium pedestals sitting on concrete, waterproof membrane, or compacted ground. No in-ground posts required. Fast and clean.

What type of framing?
The framing sits between your support system and the decking boards. Choose aluminium for longevity and PS1 certification, or timber if your builder is supplying the frame.
Timber framing
H3/H4 treated pine joists and bearers. Supplied and installed by your builder. Most common for posts-in-ground builds.
Builder supply
EconoSpan aluminium
30mm or 60mm aluminium profile. Lightweight, durable, no rot. Competitive pricing. No PS1 engineering certificate.
From $80/lm
SuperSpan aluminium
30mm, 60mm or 120mm profile. PS1 certified by EQ Struc. Required for building consent applications and elevated decks.
PS1 certified
Joist only
Joists run directly between supports. Lower profile, more Nurajack pedestals. Ideal for low-clearance builds over concrete or membrane.
Lower profile
Bearer + joist
Bearers span between supports, joists sit on top. More height but significantly fewer Nurajack pedestals. Better for uneven or sloped ground.
Fewer pedestals
