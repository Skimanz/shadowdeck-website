Engineering Certification

PS1 Producer Statement — Design

Structural engineering certification for ShadowDeck™ SuperSpan aluminium profiles. Issued by EQ Struc Limited, certified to NZ Building Code.

What is a PS1? A Producer Statement – Design (PS1) is issued by a qualified independent engineer to provide Building Consent Authorities with the reasonable grounds needed to issue a Building Consent, without duplicating the design review. This PS1 covers the structural design of ShadowDeck™ SuperSpan aluminium profiles used as joists and bearers in deck and floor structures, throughout New Zealand.

Design Features Report — ShadowDeck Joist & Bearer Floor System

1. Objective

This Design Features Report (DFR) defines the structure's design criteria and records key decisions. It outlines design loading, material properties, design standards, assumptions, and other relevant considerations, and defines the calculation procedure and checking principles.

2. Scope

Design of ShadowDeck™ SuperSpan aluminium profiles (30×60mm, 60×60mm, 120×60mm) as joists and bearers in deck and floor structures throughout New Zealand.

3. Design Standards

  • NZ Building Code — Importance Level 2 (AS/NZS 1170.0:2002)
  • Structural loading: AS/NZS 1170:2002
  • Aluminium structures: AS/NZS 1664:1997
  • Aluminium durability/surface treatment: AS/NZS 2312:2014

4. Design Loads

5. Design Methodology

  • Prepared based on reviewed drawings and client-specific demands
  • Joists and bearers designed as a continuous beam over at least three spans
  • Point load related to imposed action — maximum area of action defined

6. Durability

No acceptable solution is available for structural aluminium. Protection is provided through surface treatment as a minimum, with reference to AS/NZS 2312:2014.

7. Construction Monitoring

Design is based on verification of specific design aspects of construction, as agreed by owner or developer. All geotechnical values are based on the assumption of good ground.

8. Limitations

This document is issued for the party that commissioned it and for a specific purpose related only to the captioned project.
